ONLINE FORM SUBMISSION

Step 1 Click 'New Applicant' to register and create your own username and password.
Step 2 Login to the Passport Online Portal with the registered username and password.
Step 3 Fill in the required details in the application form and submit.
Step 4 Also Fill the Status Verification form and submit.
Step 5 Re-Enter details if any error message has been displayed after any stage of verification.
Your Passport will be issued after successful verification.
